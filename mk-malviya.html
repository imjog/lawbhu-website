Dr. Mukesh Kumar Malviya

  • About
  • Contact

B.A., LL.B. (Gold Medallist), LL.M. (Gold Medallist), M.Phil. (Law) (Gold Medallist), Ph.D., UGC-NET

Specialization - Jurisprudence, Legal Research Methodology, Indian Constitutional Law, Human Rights Law, Law & Social Transformation, Judicial Process, Law of Interpretation, Law of Evidence, Hindu Law, Muslim Law, Environmental Law, Professional Ethics and Public Interest Litigation

He obtained his LL.B. from Satpura Law College, Chhindwara, affiliated to Dr. H.S.G. University Sagar (M.P.), LL.M. and Ph.D. from the Department of Law, Barkatullah University, Bhopal (M.P.), M.Phil. (Law) from the School of Law, Devi Ahilya Viswavidhalaya, Indore (M.P.) and he obtained his Ph.D. degree for his thesis titled "भारत में सामाजिक, आर्थिक, राजनैतिक न्याय-एक विधिक अध्ययन (भारतीय संविधान में राज्य के नीति निर्देशक तत्व के विशेष सम्बन्ध में)" (2014) under the guidance of Dr. Nisha Dube, Vice Chancellor, Barkatullah University, Bhopal. He also qualified UGC-NET. He is a Gold Medallist throughout LL.B., LL.M. and M.Phil. (Law).

He joined Banaras Hindu University as an Assistant Professor of Law on 8 July 2010. He also served as an M.P. Govt. contract faculty at New Govt. Law College Indore (2009). Dr. Malviya has more than 11 years of experience of teaching of LL.B. and LL.M. classes. He has guided and supervised 1 Ph.D., more than 31 LL.M. dissertations and presently, 7 LL.M. students and 4 Ph.D. scholars are working under him.

He is the Editor-in-Chief of Jurist (an internationally referred law journal), Adhikar, 15 Days, The Public: Problem and Solution and International Research and Reviews (an internationally refereed multidisciplinary research journal). He is a member of the Editorial Board, Advisory Board and is the Legal Advisor of the many journals of law and social sciences. He is also a life member of The Indian Political Science Association, Meerut, Academique, Varanasi, Social and Legal Research Foundation, Varanasi, Vidhi Bharati Parisad, Delhi and Amnesty International Online.

He has delivered lectures in various universities and colleges, participated in number of international and national conferences, seminars, workshops, and refresher courses as a resource person. He has written many books, articles, research papers and book chapters in reputed various national and international journals, newspapers, magazines and has edited books on legal, social, economic, political, educational and environmental issues.

He has also to his credit an impressive array of over 450 high quality research papers published in prestigious national and international journals, 15 books and 88 chapters contributed to books edited by distinguished scholars and legal luminaries and has also reviewed books for journals. He has attended and presented research papers in more than 400 international and national seminars, workshops and conferences. He has delivered 80 invited lectures. He has made an indelible imprint in the fields of legal research and education.

He actively participated in N.S.S, Scout guide and Legal Literacy Camps for social welfare at various levels and was also active in student's politics as he was Student's Union President (2006) and Vice President (2005) at Satpura Law College Chhindwara (M.P.). He secured 1st position in the National Level Moot Court at Satpura Law College, Chhindwara (M.P.). He acted as convener, co-convener and member in many committees at the departmental and university level. He is an active member of the Legal Aid Clinic and anti-ragging sub-squad for 8 years. He has organized many debates and general knowledge competitions at the faculty level.
