Dr. Rudra Prakash Rai

  • About
  • Contact

B.Sc., LL.M., Ph.D.

Specialization – Commercial Laws, Business Law, Legal Aid

Professor R.P. Rai is the Head and Dean of Law School BHU. He has been teaching law since 1983 and joined BHU as a Professor of Law in November 2006. He did his LL.B. and LL.M. from the Faculty of Law, Banaras Hindu University and completed his Ph.D. in the year 1997 on “Access to Justice to the Poor with special reference to the Rewa District of Madhya Pradesh” from APS University, Rewa. Under his able guidance, 16 students have been awarded Ph.D. degrees and 12 students are presently enrolled under him. He has been a visiting faculty at the Department of History, HRD Department, MBA Department and MSW Department of APS University, Rewa. He has also been a visiting faculty at the Makhan Lal Chaturvedi University of Journalism, Bhopal. He has been the Executive Director of the prestigious Banaras Law Journal.

Aside from his teaching experience, he has been the Coordinator of the Human Rights Cell at BHU and a member of the academic councils at BHU, APS University Rewa and Barkatullah University, Bhopal and Chairman, Board of Studies, at Barkatullah University. He has also been on the Board of Studies at Hari Singh Gour University, Sagar, Tripura University, Himachal University and HN Bahuguna Garhwal University. Presently, he is a member of the Board of Studies at Pt. Ravi Shankar University, Raipur.

Additionally, Professor Rai has chaired various national and international conferences, seminars and symposiums.
