Dr. Vijay Kumar Saroj

  • About
  • Contact

LL.M., Ph.D.

Specialization - Human Rights Law, International Refugee Law, International Humanitarian Law, International Obligation

He completed his B.A. (Hons.) from the Faculty of Social Science BHU and his LL.B., LL.M. and Ph.D. from Law School BHU. He qualified NET in Law and HRD. He has published 31 research papers in national and international journals, written 5 books, written 10 chapters for other books, delivered 40 invited lectures and has presented at 70 International and National Research Seminars. In total, 28 post-graduates have been awarded LL.M. degrees under his supervision and he is currently supervising 4 Ph.D. scholars.

He is a life member of ISIL, New Delhi, a life member of the Environmental & Consumer Protection Foundation, New Delhi, a life member of Academique, a discussion group in Varanasi, a para legal volunteer at the Uttar Pradesh State Legal Service Authority and a life member of the Global Economic Progress & Research Association (Tamil Nadu).

Dr. Saroj has been awarded the following awards: 1) Rajiv Gandhi Gold Medal Award 2015 by Gepra, Tamil Nadu; 2) Global Award International: Global Brotherhood Forum (Regd.), New Delhi (28/08/2016); 3) Arch of Excellence Award, International Friendship Forum (Regd.), New Delhi (28/08/2016); 4) Certificate of Felicitation, International Friendship Forum (Regd.), New Delhi (28/08/2016); 5) Sparkling Indian Award International by the Indian Institute of Global Brotherhood, Delhi (19/12/2016); 6) Indian Global Golden Award International by the Indian Institute of Global Brotherhood, Delhi (19/12/2016); 7) Jawaharlal Nehru Gold Medal Award National by Gepra, Tamil Nadu (20/08/2016); 8) Shiksha Ratna Award by the International Social and Legal Research Foundation, Varanasi (6/12/2014); 9) Best Researcher Award by the International Social and Legal Research Foundation, Varanasi (27/12/2014); 10) Best Research Paper Award by International Social and Legal Research Foundation, Varanasi (3/01/2015); 11) Ambedkar Research Award by International Social and Legal Research Foundation, Varanasi; 12) Indira Priyadarshini Gold Medal Award by Gepra (8/03/2017); 13) Rising Son of India Award, International Friendship Forum (Regd.), New Delhi (14/05/2017); 14) Mother Teresa Global Award, International Friendship Forum (Regd.), New Delhi (14/05/2017); 15) Certificate of Felicitation, International Friendship Forum (Regd.), New Delhi (14/05/2017).
