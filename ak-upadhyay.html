Mr. Ashwini Kumar Upadhyay

  • About
  • Contact

B.A. LL.B., LL.M.

Specialization - Jurisprudence, Constitutional Law and Criminal Law

Ashwini Kumar Upadhyay did his B.A. LL.B. (Hons.) in the year 2014. He completed his LL.M. in the year 2016 from Faculty of Law, University of Allahabad, specializing in Jurisprudence, Constitutional Law and Criminal Law. During his LL.M., he secured the second rank in his batch. In the year 2016, he joined the Ph.D. Programme under the supervision of Prof. Shailendra Kumar Gupta at the Faculty of Law, BHU. The theme of his Ph.D. is “Economic Analysis of Fundamental Rights in the Constitution of India”. He has written half a dozen articles and participated in a number of seminars and workshops.

In July 2018, he got selected as an Assistant Professor at the Central University of South Bihar, Gaya. Presently, he is working as an Assistant Professor for the B.A. LL.B. Course at the Faculty of Law, BHU. Since 2017, he has also been associated with the Department of Humanistic Science, Indian Institute of Technology, BHU as a Teaching Assistant for the course of “Law and Engineering”, a prestigious interdisciplinary course designed for B.Tech. and M.Tech. students.

His area of interest includes Constitutional Law, Jurisprudence and Interpretation of Statutes. In addition to Law, he has keen interest in other disciplines, namely: Literature, Philosophy, Metaphysics and Cosmology. Creative writing, reading, meditation and listening to music are his hobbies. In the Academic Session, 2018-19 he is teaching Corporate Governance, Private International Law, and Interpretation of Statutes.
